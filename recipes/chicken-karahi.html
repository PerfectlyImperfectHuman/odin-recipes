Chicken Karahi

Home

Description

Chicken Karahi, or Kadai chicken, is undoubtedly one of the most popular curries in and out of Pakistan and India. This is a restaurant-style Pakistani Chicken Karahi recipe that can be prepared quickly and easily with no finicky steps. Tested, perfected, and loved by thousands!

Ingredients

  • Oil/Ghee
  • Onions
  • Bone-in, cut up, skinless chicken
  • Whole Spices
  • Garlic + Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Black Pepper & Garam Masala

Steps:

How to make Chicken Biryani – 3 Main Steps

  1. Heat the oil and brown the chopped onions: Sauté the onions until they’re lightly golden. Then add garlic, ginger, and green chili pepper. The onions will deepen in color as the aromatics cook.

  2. Sauté the chicken: This technique, called ‘bhunai‘ sears the meat and gives it richer flavor and color. A sprinkle of salt here helps layer the flavor.

  3. Add the tomatoes, spices, and salt: The moisture from all the tomatoes will be enough to cook the chicken while keeping it moist.

  4. Cover & cook: Allowing the chicken to slowly simmer while covered results in tender chicken and well-developed flavors. This also helps the tomatoes break down well.

  5. Uncover and stir-fry on high heat to sauté out the water content: You’re done once the chicken taking on a glossy appearance as the ghee/oil starts to separate.

  6. Stir in the yogurt:/b> I experimented with adding yogurt earlier but found I loved the pronounced taste and consistency it gives when added at the end. After adding the yogurt, continue to cook it through until the oil starts leaving the sides again.

  7. Finish off with freshly ground black peppercorns and garam masala and stir to mix. Garnish with green chili peppers, julienned ginger, and cilantro.
